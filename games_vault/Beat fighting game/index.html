🎮 TWO PLAYER

2P mode requires a CONTROLLER. P1 plays on whatever input you pick on the input screen; P2 gets the other.
On the input screen, your selection becomes P1's input and the opposite is automatically assigned to P2.

Finish your opponent with a heavy / special / ranged / ultimate blow when they're at 0 HP for a 3-second cinematic gore explosion.

🥊 BEAT KUNE DO

PARRY: Stuns opponent for 35 frames — punish freely.

TUTORIAL

👋 WELCOME TO BEAT TO BEATS

A rhythm-fighting game. Time strikes to the PULSE RING for max damage.
±60ms = PERFECT (1.5×) · ±120ms = ON-BEAT (1×) · slow = OFF-BEAT (0.5×) · way off = WHIFF (0.2×)

🥋 STEP 1 · MOVE

A/D walk · SPACE jump (×2) · S crouch · A,A or D,D dash (~25% screen, 1.4s CD).

👊 STEP 2 · ATTACK

J light · K medium · L heavy. Chains: J→K→L. Add S+attack for low strikes that hit crouched opponents. Don't spam — you have a stamina bar; mashing softens hits.

🛡 STEP 3 · DEFEND

SHIFT blocks · F parries (12-frame window, opponent stuns 2s on success). H = BURST (1× per round, escape combo). Crouch dodges high attacks.

⚡ STEP 4 · METER MOVES

U Special (25%) · O Ranged beam (50%) · I Ultimate (full meter) · G Drive Impact (50%, armored heavy) · T taunt (+meter).

🔥 STEP 5 · RAGE & FATALITY

R = RAGE MODE (HP <50%, 50% meter): every attack deals max damage for 4 seconds, ignores beat timing.
When opponent drops below 18% HP and you have 50% meter, a 💀 FATALITY cue appears above their head — press V (or all 3 attack buttons) for an instant kill.

🎮 CONTROLLER

L3 click + direction = dash · LB+RB = Drive Impact · LT+RT = Burst · L3+R3 = Rage · X+Y+B = Fatality.
